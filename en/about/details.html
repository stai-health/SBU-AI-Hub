The Power of Our University

The unique structural advantages of University of Health Sciences

University of Health Sciences has a unique structural advantage as one of Turkey's most comprehensive academic institutions in the health field. Our university has expanded its academic activities concentrated in two main campuses - the Mekteb-i Tıbbiyye-i Şâhâne (Hamidiye) Campus in Istanbul and the Gülhane Campus in Ankara - with six additional medical faculties domestically and five campuses abroad.

Health Technology City

Istanbul Pendik - Innovation Center

The Health Technology City established in Istanbul Pendik supports product development potential especially in health software and bio-medical engineering fields. This strategic location offers an ideal ecosystem for transforming academic research into practical applications.

Our Mission

SBÜ-Health Artificial Intelligence Working Group develops artificial intelligence solutions that meet critical needs in the health field, ensuring these technologies create concrete benefits in patient care.

Our Working Group transforms our university's multi-campus structure and access to a broad patient population into a strategic advantage, developing applications across a wide spectrum from diagnostic accuracy to treatment optimization, from patient monitoring to epidemic management.

In this process, we also undertake the responsibility of training competent human resources and integrating the solutions we develop into the national health system.

Our Vision

2030
Our Target Year

By 2030, we aim to create next-generation standards in health services by achieving radical improvements in patient care quality through artificial intelligence technologies.

Global Impact: Algorithms developed in our hospitals in Istanbul saving lives in health centers in Africa, research from Ankara creating value in clinics in Central Asia are concrete reflections of our vision.

Our Working Group aims to create an ecosystem that goes beyond being a technology-producing unit, ensuring the ethical and safe application of these technologies, training health professionals, and creating an ecosystem that society can safely access.

Our Strategic Goal

In this process, we will ensure that SBU becomes Turkey's leading institution in the field of artificial intelligence in health and that the knowledge we produce has a global impact.
