Our Global Research Network

National Hospital Network

Through affiliation protocols with the Turkish Public Hospitals Institution, we have access to 60 training and research hospitals, with 25 located in Istanbul, 15 in Ankara, and 18 in other cities. This extensive hospital network provides access to different demographic structures and patient populations, offering diverse clinical data sources.

International Reach

Internationally, we have active campuses in Somalia, Sudan, Philippines, and Uzbekistan, with ongoing efforts to establish new educational units in Afghanistan, Palestine, Pakistan, Syria, and Lebanon. This geographical diversity provides access to data sources critical for testing and validating artificial intelligence algorithms across different populations.

2030 Goal

Our Approach

We adopt an approach that combines academic excellence with practical solutions. Algorithms developed in our Istanbul hospitals saving lives in African health centers, and research from Ankara creating value in Central Asian clinics are concrete reflections of this approach.

Global Impact

Beyond being a technology-producing unit, we aim to create an ecosystem that ensures ethical and safe application of these technologies, trains healthcare professionals, and provides secure access for society.
