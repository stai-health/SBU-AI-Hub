About Us

The SBU-Health AI Working Group is a research group within Turkey's largest health university, the Health Sciences University, conducting research in health and artificial intelligence. Our goal is to effectively use AI technologies in healthcare to revolutionize diagnosis, treatment, and patient care.

Our Mission

To develop AI applications in healthcare that enable early diagnosis of diseases, improve treatment processes, and increase the efficiency of healthcare systems.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in AI applications in healthcare in Turkey and worldwide and to become an internationally recognized center of excellence in this field.

Our Research Areas

We conduct research in various fields to develop AI applications in healthcare. Here are our key research focus areas:

Medical Imaging and Diagnostic Algorithms AI Working Group

We develop AI-assisted image analysis and diagnostic systems in radiology, pathology, and ophthalmology.

More Info

Clinical Decision Support Systems AI Working Group

We develop data-driven decision support systems to assist physicians in diagnosis and treatment processes.

More Info

Biomedical Signal Processing and Monitoring AI Working Group

We develop continuous patient monitoring and early warning systems by analyzing physiological signals like ECG, EEG, and EMG using AI methods.

More Info

Health Data Science and Big Data Analytics AI Working Group

We analyze large health datasets to identify disease trends and conduct epidemiological studies.

More Info

Genomics, Bioinformatics and Personalized Medicine AI Working Group

We develop personalized medicine applications through the analysis of genetic data.

More Info

Digital Health, Smart Systems, Wearable and Biomedical Technologies AI Working Group

We develop accessible healthcare services through remote patient monitoring, telemedicine applications, and digital health platforms.

More Info

Drug Discovery and Pharmacology AI Working Group

We work on AI applications for the discovery and development of new drug molecules.

More Info

Public Health and Epidemiology AI Working Group

We analyze public health dynamics using AI to support epidemic prediction, disease spread modeling, and public health intervention planning.

More Info

Medical Education, Simulation and Virtual Reality AI Working Group

We develop AI-based training platforms, simulation systems, and personalized learning solutions for medical students and healthcare professionals.

More Info

AI Ethics, Law and Policies in Healthcare AI Working Group

We develop ethical principles, legal regulations, and policy recommendations to ensure the safe, fair, and transparent use of AI technologies in healthcare.
