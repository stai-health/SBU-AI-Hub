About Us

SBÜ-Health Artificial Intelligence Working Group is a research group within the largest health university in Turkey, Sağlık Bilimleri Üniversitesi, conducting research in the fields of health and artificial intelligence. Our goal is to revolutionize diagnosis, treatment, and patient care by effectively utilizing artificial intelligence technologies in healthcare.

Our Mission

To develop artificial intelligence applications in healthcare, enabling early diagnosis of diseases, improving treatment processes, and increasing the efficiency of health systems.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in artificial intelligence applications in healthcare in Turkey and worldwide, and to become a center of excellence recognized at the international level in this field.
