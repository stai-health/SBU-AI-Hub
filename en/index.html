About Us

SBÜ-Health Artificial Intelligence Research Group is a group conducting research in health and artificial intelligence within Turkey's largest health university, Sağlık Bilimleri Üniversitesi. Our goal is to revolutionize diagnosis, treatment, and patient care by effectively using artificial intelligence technologies in healthcare.

Our Mission

To develop artificial intelligence applications in healthcare services, ensuring early diagnosis of diseases, improving treatment processes, and increasing the efficiency of health systems.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in artificial intelligence applications in healthcare in Turkey and worldwide, and to become a center of excellence recognized at the international level in this field.

Our Research Areas

We conduct research in various fields to develop artificial intelligence applications in healthcare. Here are the main research areas we focus on:

Medical Imaging and Diagnostic Algorithms AI Working Group

We develop AI-assisted image analysis and diagnostic systems in radiology, pathology, and ophthalmology fields.

Detailed Information

Clinical Decision Support Systems AI Working Group

We develop data-driven decision support systems that assist physicians in diagnosis and treatment processes.

Detailed Information

Biomedical Signal Processing and Monitoring AI Working Group

We develop continuous patient monitoring and early warning systems by analyzing physiological signals such as ECG, EEG, EMG with artificial intelligence methods.

Detailed Information

Health Data Science and Big Data Analytics AI Working Group

We conduct epidemiological studies and determine disease trends by analyzing large health data.

Detailed Information

Genomics, Bioinformatics and Personalized Medicine AI Working Group

We develop personalized medicine applications through the analysis of genetic data.

Detailed Information

Digital Health, Smart Systems, Wearable and Biomedical Technologies AI Working Group

We develop accessible healthcare services through remote patient monitoring, telemedicine applications, and digital health platforms.

Detailed Information

Drug Discovery and Pharmacology AI Working Group

We work on artificial intelligence applications in the discovery and development of new drug molecules.

Detailed Information

Public Health and Epidemiology AI Working Group

We support epidemic prediction, disease spread modeling, and planning of public health interventions by analyzing dynamics affecting community health with artificial intelligence.

Detailed Information

Medical Education, Simulation and Virtual Reality AI Working Group

We develop AI-based educational platforms, simulation systems, and personalized learning solutions for medical students and healthcare professionals.

Detailed Information

AI Ethics, Law and Policy in Healthcare AI Working Group

We develop ethical principles, legal regulations, and policy recommendations to ensure the safe, fair, and transparent use of artificial intelligence technologies in healthcare.

Detailed Information

Our Recent Publications

Recent scientific studies published by our research team in health and artificial intelligence:

Deep Learning for Automated Detection of Diabetic Retinopathy in Fundus Images: A Comparative Study

Özkan M, Demir Z, Yılmaz A, Kaya A. Journal of Medical Artificial Intelligence, 2024; 15(3): 245-258.

In this study, the performance of different deep learning models in diabetic retinopathy detection was compared, and a new hybrid model was proposed especially for early-stage detection.

AI-Based Prediction of COVID-19 Severity from Chest CT Images: A Multi-Center Study in Turkey

Yılmaz A, Kaya A, Arslan M, Yıldız S. European Journal of Radiology, 2023; 152: 110358.

In a multi-center study in Turkey, an artificial intelligence model was developed to predict disease severity from chest CT images of COVID-19 patients.
